About

Olli-Pekka Hietto (a.k.a. Foreign Mechanics) is a Finnish sound artist working with visual media and installations.

His work explores and uses both the analog and digital realm of sound. His strong aesthetic and conceptual methods are at the core of his creative process.

Olli-Pekka is currently a Bachelor student in Berlin studying electronic music production and performance
